Protocols

V2Ray supports the following protocols. Each protocol may be an inbound protocol, or an outbound protocol, or both.

  • Dokodemo-door
  • Freedom
  • HTTP
  • MTProto
  • Shadowsocks
  • Socks
  • VMess
